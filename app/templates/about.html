Overview


The inKwave is an innovative application designed to provide users with a digital canvas for creating artwork. Utilizing advanced computer vision and graphics algorithms, this system allows users to simulate traditional painting techniques in a virtual environment. The main objective of the project is to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that mimics the experience of real-world painting, enabling artists to explore their creativity without the constraints of physical media.
